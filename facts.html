MORE FACTS:

Memex is located on Stanford University's campus and hosted by the Stanford Research Computing Facility (SRCF). The SRCF data center provides secured access, 24/7 monitoring and assistance, controlled temperature within ASHREA A1 parameters, sufficient power and UPS, backup generators and fire detection/suppression in case of fire.  The SRCF has multiple redundant 10 gigabit networks linking to the Stanford campus backbone, the Internet, and other national networks. Connectivity at 100 gigabits to Intenet2 and the CENIC regional network is in place, connecting the building to national and California Universities at very high speed. Connectivity to the Memex cluster from the Internet and from our Stanford departments is provided via redundant 10 gigabit links.  Memex also uses SLURM as its main job scheduler and Intel/GCC compilers work in tangent with OpenMPI, OpenMP, and several research software packages and libraries.
